About InjuryPredictionML

InjuryPredictionML is an advanced machine learning system designed to predict sports injuries and estimate recovery times. Our model uses state-of-the-art algorithms including Random Forest, XGBoost, and LightGBM to analyze various player metrics such as age, weight, height, training intensity, sleep patterns, hydration levels, and muscle fatigue.

This project leverages comprehensive data analysis and machine learning techniques to help athletes, coaches, and sports professionals make informed decisions about training schedules, injury prevention, and recovery planning.
